Contribute data to the Human Cell Atlas beta

This is the space where you organise your data for submission to the Human Cell Atlas data coordination platform.

Register a Project

Start the data contribution process by registering a project for the HCA data coordination platform.

  • Enter basic project information.
  • Receive a metadata spreadsheet.
  • Get support by our data wranglers.

My HCA Projects

Work on your projects and datasets in your own secure area until you're ready to submit them.

  • Add and Edit your projects.
  • Upload a metadata spreadsheet.
  • Get projects published in the HCA Data Portal.

This is a beta service, and we are constantly improving it.
Get in touch with our Data Wranglers with any questions about data submission: wrangler-team@data.humancellatlas.org.
